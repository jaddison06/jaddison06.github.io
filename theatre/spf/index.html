"School Play Festival" (Sep 2023) | design | technical management | rigging | programming

The School Play Festival took place every 5 years at my school. A collection of student-written theatre pieces are performed over the course of a week, across all 4 of our venues. The 2023 Festival featured several comedies, a wartime thriller, a musical and a dance piece.

As well as designing lighting for the dance piece (pictured), my role was to coordinate the lighting department for the whole festival, making sure crew were confident rigging, focusing, programming and operating. I ran workshops in the leadup to tech week, covering all the key elements of lighting design & programming, and was on hand throughout the tech process to supervise, keep track of progress and troubleshoot if needed on every show. I also helped out in the sound department, teaching younger students Qlab techniques & live mixing, and working on the musical as #2.
