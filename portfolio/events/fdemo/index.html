"F Demo" (Sep 2023) | direction | design | rigging | programming

Back at school, the Year 13 heads of theatre every year got the opportunity to present to new Year 9s to show them what we do and how they can join in. I'd always felt that more could be done with the format - showing them why we do what we do instead of just telling them. In my final year I was able to create a new show - telling the story of how we as theatre makers tell stories. I came up with the concept for the production, wrote the script, designed the sound and lighting and directed the staging.
