"Live Events '26" (Feb 2026) | design | rigging | programming

Rose Bruford's Live Events project is one of the highlights of the course.

The Second Year cohort conceptualised, designed and came together to build a rig for live music in our Rose Theatre. Each student then pre-visualised and programmed a timecode piece in the GrandMA3 software.

This was a massively rewarding project for us to put into practice all the skills we've developed to be able to independently realise a full concert experience.
