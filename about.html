| about

Jas Addison is a visual and lighting designer for live events, theatre, and screen. Passionate about integrating technologies for a seamless and precise audience experience, Jas has over 9 years of experience in lighting design, rigging and programming, network systems, and video design and programming.

Jas' main passion is concert & live event design, but their extensive portfolio also covers theatre and film work.
